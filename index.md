Hi, I’m an incoming EECS PhD student at Massachusetts Institute of Technology. This summer, I’m doing continual learning research at DeepSeek. Previously, I was a research fellow at Anthropic. I received my BA and MS from University of California, Berkeley, where I worked with Dawn Song.

I’m broadly interested in LLM safety and post-training. Lately, I’ve been thinking about how to get denser reward signals from RL rollouts, and how to make LLMs better at AI research itself.
